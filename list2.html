Best coffee shops in Columbia, Missouri

Coffee has become an art and a business in every city a person can go to. With larger chains like Starbucks popping up at every street corner, it is becoming harder to support local coffee shops. This list is to show the seven best coffee shops around Columbia to either get a fix of good drinks or to just study and hang out with friends.

Lakota Coffee Company

Lakota Coffee features a wide array of coffee and tea drinks as well as a comforting atmosphere for studying right downtown only a few blocks away from Mizzou. Located at 24 South Ninth Street, Columbia, MO 65201. (573) 874-2852(573) 874-2852
