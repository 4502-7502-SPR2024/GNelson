Best coffee shops in Columbia, Missouri

Coffee has become an art and a business in every city a person can go to. With larger chains like Starbucks popping up at every street corner, it is becoming harder to support local coffee shops. This list is to show the seven best coffee shops around Columbia to either get a fix of good drinks or to just study and hang out with friends.

Lakota Coffee Company

Lakota Coffee features a wide array of coffee and tea drinks as well as a comforting atmosphere for studying right downtown only a few blocks away from Mizzou. 24 South Ninth Street, Columbia, MO 65201. (573) 874-2852(573) 874-2852

Shortwave Coffee

Shortwave features a variety of fresh foods and freshly brewed coffee that the business owners have handcrafted to create the perfect blend. 915 Alley A, Columbia, MO 65201 (573)214-0880

The Grind Coffee House

The Grind has locations near and on Mizzou’s campus with speedy service that is perfect for a quick grab and go coffee and snacks. 311 S 9th St, Columbia, MO 65201 (573)447-3333

Love Coffee

Love Coffee is coffee with a purpose in the community with their goal to help train those with disabilities in customer service in addition to providing great drinks. 15 Business Loop 70 E Columbia, MO 65203 (573)777-1877

Fretboard Coffee

Fretbaord serves cheap coffee that is brewed fresh in front of you in addition to a lovely atmosphere you can hang out and chill in. 1013 E Walnut St Columbia, MO 65201 (573)227-2233

Travellin Tom’s Coffee Trucky

Although not a usual place to get coffee, this traveling coffee truck serves iced and warm drinks all around Columbia wherever they park their truck to serve, making the experience fun and mobile.

Scooters

Despite being a chain, this coffee place brings drinks to the next level with ice cream and speedy service in small buildings that make you feel at home.2501 Grindstone Pkwy Columbia, MO 65201
